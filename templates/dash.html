Latin N-Gram Viewer

Latin N-Gram Viewer is a simple tool to search n-grams within a selected corpus of ancient Latin texts and visualize their frequency

All you need to do is type a text string into the search bar in the top right-hand corner

For multiple searching use a comma to separate the n-grams
